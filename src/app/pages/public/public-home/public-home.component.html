Farmlab

Farmlab is a hydrophonics based farm. This means that the plants are not put in soil but in water. The plants will grow in the best possible circumstances. Everything in the farm is monitored through sensors. All this information is shown in an online platform where you can monitor your farms and also change the grow plans.

Monitoring

  • - growth of the plant
  • - waterlevel of the plant reservoir
  • - light intensity
  • - ph value of the water
  • - nutritien of the plants

The farm is monitored with smart sensors so we can see the realtime value of all monitored objects.

Hydroponics

  • - planted in water
  • - more optimal growing conditions
  • - better monitoring of the plants

Hydroponics is a growing technique where you put the plants in water instead of soil. The plants need less effort to get there nutritients to grow optimal.

Controlling

  • - water flow
  • - nutritien flow
  • - light intensity
  • - plant programs

You can control/adjust the farm actuator values. Change the plan of your farm to a different grow plan for other plants.
